Training and Technical Team

 
Gigi Crowder, L.E.
 
Min. Monique Tarver
 
Pastor Horacio Jones
 

The MHFC Team, Gigi Crowder, Min. Monique Tarver and Pastor Horacio Jones, has worked with over 50 African American Congregations, over 10 County Behavioral Health Departments impacting at least 20,000 persons and are recognized as subject matter experts by consumer and family groups,community stakeholders, Behavioral Health Care Services and African American faith leaders throughout California. They bring a unique perspective that enable participants to carefully and compassionately navigate through the intercection of faith/spirituality and mental wellness for the African American community.
